The document proposes the **Bionic Vision System**, a low-cost wearable assistive solution designed to improve independent mobility for visually impaired individuals. It addresses limitations of existing mobility aids by integrating micro-level hazard detection with macro-level route guidance, using computer vision, depth estimation, OCR, and voice feedback while performing all AI processing on the mobile device for low-latency, offline operation.
